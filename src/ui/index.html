Getting started

Airmonlink Composer Help

Enter music

Select a part and duration, then move the pointer over the staff. A faint ghost note shows the exact pitch and beat; red means it cannot fit in that bar. Click to place it. Drag existing notes vertically or horizontally within the same clef.

Keyboard shortcuts

Space play or stop · Ctrl+S save · Ctrl+Z undo · Delete remove selected note · 1–7 choose whole through sixty-fourth durations · Shift+click adds a chord note.

Harmony and sol-fa

Enter a melody on any selected staff and layer, choose Harmonize, then choose whether the harmony should use layers on that staff, the same piano grand staff, SATB staves, or newly added staves. Tonic sol-fa and lyrics update automatically.
